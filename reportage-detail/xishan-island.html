Xishan Island

Suzhou, Jiangsu | December 2024

Xishan Island in Taihu Lake is a peaceful retreat known for its natural beauty and serene landscapes. The island is also famous for its Bi Luo Chun tea plantations, producing one of China’s most celebrated green teas. Visitors come to enjoy both the tranquil scenery and the rich tea culture that defines the island.
