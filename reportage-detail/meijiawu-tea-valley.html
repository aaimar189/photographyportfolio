Meijiawu Tea Valley

Hangzhou, Zhejiang | April 2025

Meijiawu Village, near Hangzhou, is famous for its Longjing (Dragon Well) tea, prized for its delicate aroma and smooth, nutty flavor. The 2025 harvest typically begins in early April, when the first tender leaves are picked for the highest quality tea. Visiting the village offers a chance to see traditional tea cultivation and enjoy the scenic terraces where this iconic Chinese tea is grown.
