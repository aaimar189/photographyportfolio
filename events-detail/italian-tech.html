Sam Altman at the Italian Tech Week

Torino, Italy | October 2024

I photographed Sam Altman and John Elkann, chairman of Stellantis, at Italian Tech Week 2024 in Turin. Italian Tech Week is a leading technology conference where founders, investors, and industry leaders gather to discuss innovation and future trends. The 2024 edition offered a key opportunity to see two influential figures shaping the tech and automotive worlds.
