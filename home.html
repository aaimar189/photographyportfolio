Who I Am

I’m an Italian photographer with a deep passion for editorial and cultural photography. I focus on capturing the finer details that bring stories to life, always aiming to create images that resonate and leave a lasting impression.

Where I Am Based

I've been living in China for eight years and am based in Shanghai, the country's dynamic economic hub. For the past two years, I've been capturing the essence of this vibrant city and traveling across China to document its diverse landscapes and cultures.
